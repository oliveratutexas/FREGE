FREGE

Fundamentals of Reasoning for the Electronic Age

What is FREGE?

FREGE (Fundamentals of Reasoning for the Electronic Age) is a pair of introductory "courses" that cover the topics of Reasoning(Logic) and Discrete Mathematics (Sets, Relations and Functions).FREGE's target audience is beginning computer science students, but the Reasoning course is also appropriate for students in other disciplines who want to learn the art of clear thinking.

Who is Involved in FREGE?

FREGE is a free online education initiaitve started by the University of Texas at Austin under the guide of Doctor Elaine Rich and Doctor Alan Cline, with student contributions from the Empowering Leadership Alliance (ELA) and help from students in the UTCS program.
